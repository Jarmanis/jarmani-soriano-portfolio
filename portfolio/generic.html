About Me

As a recent graduate with honors from Correlation One's 17-week Software Development training program, I can confidently say that this bootcamp transformed my skills and career prospects. The training, facilitated through Codio, provided in-depth knowledge of Advanced HTML/CSS, UI/UX Design, and JavaScript Frameworks. The curriculum was designed around solving real-world, case-based business problems, which made learning incredibly practical and relevant.

One of the most rewarding aspects was developing web applications using React.js. The component-based architecture we learned was a game-changer for UI development, allowing us to create dynamic and efficient user interfaces. The hands-on approach, combined with the use of real-world data, ensured that we were not just learning theory but applying our skills in meaningful ways.

The support from instructors and the collaborative environment with fellow students fostered a rich learning experience. Graduating with honors from Correlation One has opened numerous opportunities for me in the tech industry, and I now feel well-equipped to tackle complex projects and contribute effectively to any development team.

As a Transportation Associate for Amazon, I've honed my skills in navigating bustling logistics environments with precision and efficiency. Operating within the heart of the supply chain, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a knack for problem-solving under pressure. Whether orchestrating the seamless movement of trailers or optimizing space utilization, my experience has instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility and adaptability, crucial traits in both the fast-paced world of e-commerce and the dynamic field of software development.
